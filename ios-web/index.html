How to use Lift-O-Graph
  1. Pick a training day from the dropdown, such as Push Day, Pull Day, or Leg Day.
  2. Tap an exercise chip, or use the + button in Exercises to add one.
  3. Use the Manage training days list to remove a specific day.
  4. Enter weight and reps, then tap Log set.
  5. For a whole workout, type something like bench press 145 for 6 and 145 for 8, curls 30 for 12, then tap Log full workout.
  6. Use the Weight, Reps, Est. 1RM, and Volume chips to change the graph.

The box under Training Day adds a new training day. Exercises are the chips in the Exercises section.
